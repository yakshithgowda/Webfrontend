Murdeshwar is a town in Uttara Kannada district in the state of Karnataka, India, It is famous for the world's second tallest Shiva statue, the town lies on the coast of the Arabian Sea.

Maravanthe

It takes 2 hours, 9 minutes to travel from Mangalore to Maravanthe Beach. Approximate driving distance between Mangalore and Maravanthe Beach is 108 kms or 67.1 miles or 58.3 nautical miles.

St. Mary's

St. Mary's viewpoint in Mangalore offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and the Arabian Sea. Situated atop a hill, it's a serene spot for reflection and sightseeing. The viewpoint is also home to the beautiful St. Mary's Island, known for its unique basalt rock formations and pristine beaches.

Location

Pilikula

Pilikula Nisargadhama, located in Mangalore, is a popular eco-tourism destination. It features a stunning botanical garden, zoo, boating facilities, and a lake. Visitors can explore a science center, a heritage village showcasing local culture, and an exhilarating adventure park.

Location

Kadri Park

Kadri Park is a picturesque urban green space. It's famous for its historic Kadri Manjunath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The park includes a beautifully landscaped garden, a children's play area, and a musical fountain. Kadri Park is a tranquil oasis within the bustling city, perfect for relaxation and recreation.
